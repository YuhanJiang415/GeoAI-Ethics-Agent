How to score — three axes, pass / fail per triple

1 · Groundedness Is this triple really supported by the audited paper? The head (technical feature / data choice) genuinely belongs to this paper, and the cited verbatim quote exists in the text and supports it. Fail = hallucination / mis-attribution. → grounding precision. 2 · Causal validity Is head → relation → tail a sound, defensible ethical causal claim (true in the domain-KG sense, not a non-sequitur)? Fail = the causal link itself is wrong. → edge precision. 3 · Instantiation verdict Is INSTANTIATED / POTENTIAL / NOT_TRIGGERED correct for this paper? Fail = e.g. a merely "potential" risk marked as already realized, or vice-versa. → verdict precision.
