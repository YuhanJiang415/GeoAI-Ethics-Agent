Dual-Track GeoAI Ethics Auditor

An offline build distills GeoAI-ethics papers into a causal knowledge graph (Track 1) and a vector store (Track 2). An online agent audits any new paper against both and emits machine-gradable causal triples plus a prose report. Two visualizations below.
